The Experience

The three-day program mirrors the personalized, self-directed experience of our students and teachers, and is designed for teachers and school leaders who are interested in implementing a Next Generation learning model. You will leave with a 2015-2016 change management plan that will prepare you to launch in 2016-2017.

Over the course of the program, attendees will dive deeper into the elements of a Next Generation school model, including:

  • Role of the Student
  • Role of the Teacher
  • Role of the Parent
  • Role of the School Leader
  • Change Management Process
  • Self-Directed Learning Culture
